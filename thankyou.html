Who This Call Is and Is Not For

This call is for you if:

  • ✓ You run marketing at a B2B SaaS company doing $30M+ ARR (sweet spot: $50M+)
  • ✓ Your average deal size is $25K+ ACV and you have a $50K+/month paid media budget
  • ✓ You're generating MQLs but pipeline isn't growing, and sales doesn't trust the leads marketing sends over
  • ✓ You're ready to invest $20K-$31K/month in a demand gen partner within the next 30-60 days
  • ✓ You (or your VP/CMO/CRO) can make the decision to move forward on the call

This call is not for you if:

  • ✕ You're under $30M ARR or your ACV is below $25K (the system requires a minimum scale to deliver ROI)
  • ✕ You're looking for someone to run a few LinkedIn ads or manage a single campaign
  • ✕ You don't have a dedicated sales team to follow up on qualified pipeline
  • ✕ You expect qualified pipeline growth in 30 days from a standing start (realistic timeline is 90-180 days)
  • ✕ The decision-maker can't join the call

If any of the "not for you" criteria apply, please reschedule. This call won't be the right fit, and we'd rather save both of our time. Reschedule here.
